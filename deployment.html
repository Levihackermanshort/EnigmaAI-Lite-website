How Enigma AI Will Be Delivered and Supported

Deployment

Initial deployment will target local execution environments, followed by containerized Docker builds and optional cloud integrations. For academic and small-business applications, self-hosting and secure REST APIs will be supported. Long-term, Enigma AI may evolve into a platform-as-a-service (PaaS) with customizable modules.
