Vision & Objectives

Vision

Develop a user-focused AI assistant with domain-specific capabilities, ensuring ethical and transparent AI design using open-source technologies. Enigma AI aims to provide a testing platform for adaptive interfaces and dialogue personalization, while investigating efficient model inference and modular integration techniques.

Objectives

  • Develop a user-focused AI assistant with domain-specific capabilities.
  • Ensure ethical and transparent AI design using open-source technologies.
  • Provide a testing platform for adaptive interfaces and dialogue personalization.
  • Investigate efficient model inference and modular integration techniques.
