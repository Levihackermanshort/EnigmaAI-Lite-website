Why Enigma AI? Understanding the Need for Transparent, Ethical AI

Background

Large Language Models like GPT-4 and Claude have demonstrated impressive conversational performance but pose limitations in terms of opacity, scalability, and control. Enigma AI addresses these challenges by focusing on a lightweight, transparent alternative suitable for local deployment and academic experimentation.

Motivation

Enigma AI is motivated by the need for transparent, controllable, and privacy-aware AI systems that can be easily adapted for academic and professional use. The project aims to empower users and researchers with a platform that prioritizes ethical design and user-centric features.
